Operational notes and deployment checks for Chopsticks + Agents.

Operational notes

  • Lavalink required for audio playback.
  • One Agent equals one concurrent voice session per server.
  • Multiple active channels require multiple Agents.

Checklist

  • Main bot invited
  • Agents invited (match desired concurrency)
  • Lavalink reachable (host/port/password correct)
  • Agents online (tokens valid, runner connected)
  • Playback verified in at least one voice channel
  • Multi-channel verified by deploying at least two Agents
